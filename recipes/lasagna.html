Lasagna

Description:

Homemade lasagan may have several steps, but each step is easy and worth the time in order to make this delicious Italian-American dish.

This is an easy to follow lasagan recipe that will require only one baking dish, one bowl and a pan!

Ingrediants:

  • Cheese

    • For classic lasagna, the recipe calls for ricotta and parmasian. If no ricotta, cottage cheese will work fine.

  • Meat

    • I recomend both Italian sausage and ground beef

  • Sauce

    • To keep it quick, just use pasta sauce or marina sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
